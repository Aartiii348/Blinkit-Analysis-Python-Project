**2. Case Statement**

Blinkit, an online grocery delivery platform, maintains large product-level datasets containing information such as item type, fat content, MRP, ratings, outlet size, outlet location, and establishment year.
However, the company needs a structured analysis to understand:

Which product categories generate the highest sales

How fat content affects customer purchases

Which outlets perform better and why

How location and size influence revenue

What factors impact customer ratings and product demand
